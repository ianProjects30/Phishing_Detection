1.1 Introduction to Phishing

Phishing is a malicious attempt to obtain sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details, often for malicious reasons, by disguising as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication. It is one of the most common cyber threats today.

PhishGuard offers a robust, real-time defense system. It employs **machine learning models** and a continuously updated database of known malicious domains to analyze URLs instantaneously, giving you a verdict of **Safe** or **Phishing Detected**.

1.2 Quick Start Guide

To use PhishGuard's primary detection tool:

  1. **Copy** the entire suspicious URL (including the `http://` or `https://` part).
  2. Navigate to the **Home** screen (URL Detection page).
  3. **Paste** the URL into the input field.
  4. Click the **'Check here'** button.
  5. The result will be displayed instantly below the input area.

2.1 Running a URL Check

The URL check uses a multi-layered analysis technique. We examine several factors, including the link's encoding, domain registration details, certificate validity, and content similarity to known phishing pages. The entire process takes less than a second.

2.2 Interpreting Results

  • SAFE: The URL analysis did not find any known malicious signs. It is generally safe to proceed, but always exercise caution.
  • PHISHING DETECTED: The URL exhibits strong characteristics of a phishing or malicious site. **DO NOT** click or proceed to this website.
  • UNCERTAIN: (Rare) The URL has unusual characteristics that prevent a clear verdict. Treat this result with extreme caution and avoid interacting with the site.

3.1 Using PhishMail

PhishMail allows you to securely analyze suspicious emails without opening them or clicking any links.

3.2 Reviewing History

The History section logs every URL you have submitted for checking. This is useful for:

  • Reviewing past threats you've encountered.
  • Tracking domains you frequently check.
  • Confirming the safety status of a link checked earlier.

4.1 FAQs and Support

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. We prioritize your privacy. The URLs you submit are used only for analysis and model improvement. We do not store any personally identifiable information (PII) linked to your history.
